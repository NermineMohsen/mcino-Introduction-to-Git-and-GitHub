1. Our Pledge
   Participants in the community pledge to create a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of various personal characteristics, emphasizing an open, welcoming, diverse, and healthy community.
2. Our Standards
   This section defines acceptable and unacceptable behavior. Positive behaviors include empathy, respect, and constructive feedback, while unacceptable behaviors include sexualized language, harassment, and doxxing.
3. Enforcement Responsibilities
   Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards and have the right to remove contributions that do not align with the code.
4. Scope
   The code of conduct applies within community spaces and when representing the community in public spaces.
5. Enforcement
   Violations can be reported to community leaders, who review and investigate complaints promptly and confidentially.
6. Enforcement Guidelines
   Community leaders use guidelines to determine consequences, which can range from a private warning to a permanent ban.
7. Attribution
